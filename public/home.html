Hamilton Farm Facts

  • Founded in 1911, Hamilton Farm is a 730-acre estate located in Bedminster, New Jersey
  • 2012 & 2021 NJ PGA Club of the Year
  • The Hickory Course, with a full eighteen holes, is the only USGA rated par 3 course in America
  • The United States Equestrian team headquarters is located at Hamilton Farm, where they use it to train for the Olympic Games
  • The course was created in 2001 by Dana Fry and recently was renovated in 2021 with the 13th and 15th holes recieving the biggest changes.
